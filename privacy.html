Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us


Last Updated: November 28, 2025

Introduction

Isaac Pehrson ("I", "me", or "my") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal data that may be collected. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit my website or use any of my applications, including Chrome extensions.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site or use my applications.

Data Collection

Currently, I do not collect any personal user data.

My website and applications are designed to function without requiring any personal information from you. I do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (such as name, address, email address, or phone number)
  • Financial information (such as credit card numbers)
  • Health information
  • Authentication information (such as passwords or credentials)
  • Personal communications (such as emails or messages)
  • Location data
  • Web browsing history
  • User activity (such as network monitoring, clicks, mouse position, or scroll)
  • Website content or data entered by users

Third-Party Services

My website and applications do not share any user data with third parties. Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to share, sell, or transfer to any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics providers.

Chrome Extensions

Any Chrome extensions I develop operate entirely locally on your device. They are designed with privacy in mind and:

  • Do not collect or transmit any user data
  • Do not access your browsing history
  • Do not track your online activities
  • Do not use remote code execution
  • Only request permissions that are necessary for their core functionality

If any future extension requires data collection, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and you will be notified of any changes before such collection begins.

Data Security

Although no personal data is currently collected, I am committed to implementing appropriate security measures should data collection become necessary in the future. Any future data handling will adhere to industry best practices and applicable data protection regulations.

Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is currently no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, if you have any concerns or questions about your privacy, you are welcome to contact me.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in my practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. I will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. I encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how I am protecting your information.
